✦ THE 5 IRON RULES — NEVER BREAK
01
Sector Before Stock
The macro tailwind must be 15+ years long. Only then pick the best business inside that sector. Never reverse this order.
02
Let Winners Run Ruthlessly
Jhunjhunwala held Titan for 20 years through multiple crashes. Your best stock will likely double your wealth. Never sell quality on a valuation argument alone.
03
Conviction Over Diversification
35 high-conviction stocks beats 70 mediocre ones. If you can't explain why you own something in 2 sentences — sell it and buy more of what you know.
04
Volatility ≠ Risk
A 35% drawdown in a fundamentally sound business is an opportunity, not a warning. Permanent capital loss is risk. Price fluctuation is noise.
05
Protect with Cash
Always keep 2–5% as dry powder. The best investments come during panic. Without cash, you're just a passenger during corrections, not an investor.
06
Management First
A great sector with a dishonest promoter is a trap. Screen for promoter integrity, capital allocation history, and pledge-free holdings before anything else.
